K.E. Carmel School

Admissions

Admissions are open for the upcoming academic session. The process is simple and transparent, ensuring equal opportunity for all applicants.

Process
  1. Fill the admission form
  2. Submit required documents
  3. Interaction/assessment (if required)
  4. Confirmation after verification

Admission Process

  1. Collect and fill the admission form
  2. Submit required documents to the school office
  3. Interaction/basic assessment for higher classes (if required)
  4. Admission confirmed after verification and approval

Admissions are granted based on availability of seats and fulfillment of basic requirements.

Classes for Admission

  • Nursery and Kindergarten
  • Primary Classes
  • Middle School
  • Secondary Classes (up to Class X)

Seats are limited and subject to availability.

Documents Required

  • Birth Certificate (age proof)
  • Previous school report card
  • Transfer Certificate (for students from other schools)
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Any other documents as required by the school

Schedule & Guidelines

  • Admissions usually open before the start of the academic session
  • Apply early due to limited seats
  • Exact dates and updates will be announced by the school

School hours may vary by class level. Office hours are typically during working days for admission inquiries. Please contact the school office for exact timings.

  • Ensure all documents are complete at the time of submission
  • Provide accurate information in the application form
  • Follow instructions given by the school office
Need assistance? For admission-related queries, parents can visit the school campus or contact the school office during working hours. Our staff will guide you through the process.
