About K.E. Carmel School

Quality Education with Strong Values

School Overview

K.E. Carmel School, Siliguri is a well-established co-educational institution dedicated to providing quality education in a disciplined and nurturing environment. The school focuses on building a strong academic foundation while shaping character, values, and life skills in every student.

Located in Siliguri, West Bengal, the school follows a balanced approach to education, combining academics with co-curricular and extracurricular activities to ensure the overall development of students.

Why Choose K.E. Carmel School?

  • Focus on academic excellence and conceptual learning
  • Emphasis on discipline, values, and character building
  • Safe and supportive learning environment
  • Equal importance to academics and co-curricular activities
  • Experienced and dedicated teaching staff
